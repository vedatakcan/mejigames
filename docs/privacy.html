Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 25, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Meji: Brain Training (“we”, “our”, or “us”) handles information when you use our mobile application (“Application”), available on both Apple App Store (iOS) and Google Play Store (Android).

This policy applies equally to all users, regardless of platform. By using the Application, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process personally identifiable information such as name, email address, phone number, or contact details.

All gameplay progress, scores, preferences, and settings are stored locally on your device only via a Room database. This data is never transmitted to our servers and cannot be accessed by the Developer.

2. Third-Party Services

The Application uses third-party services that may collect data independently:

  • Google AdMob – Used for displaying advertisements.
    AdMob may collect advertising IDs, device identifiers, and usage-related data.
    Google AdMob Privacy Policy

3. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

Apple App Store (iOS): Purchases are processed through Apple In-App Purchase. Payment details are handled exclusively by Apple.

Google Play Store (Android): Purchases are processed through Google Play Billing. The Developer does not have access to payment information.

4. Data Security

All application data stored locally remains on your device. If the Application is uninstalled, this data may be permanently lost.

5. Children’s Privacy

The Application does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Since no personal data is collected directly, the Application complies with COPPA and similar regulations.
