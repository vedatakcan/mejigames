Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 5, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Meji: Brain Training (“we,” “our,” or “us”) handles information when you use our mobile application (“Application”), available on both Apple App Store (iOS) and Google Play Store (Android).

This Privacy Policy applies to all users regardless of platform. By using the Application, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process personally identifiable information (PII) such as name, email address, phone number, or contact details.

All gameplay progress, scores, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device only. This data is never uploaded to our servers and cannot be accessed or restored by the Developer.

We may collect limited non-personal information automatically for functionality, analytics, advertising, and performance purposes, including:

  • Diagnostics: Crash logs, stability metrics, and performance data

The Developer does not sell or share personal data with third parties. Third-party services operate under their own privacy policies.

3. Data Security

Local application data is stored only on your device. If you uninstall the Application or change devices, this data may be permanently lost. The Developer cannot recover or restore local data.

Any data processed by third-party providers is protected according to their own security practices.

Local Data: Retained until the Application is uninstalled or app data is cleared.

Third-Party Data: Retained in accordance with Apple, Google, or advertising providers’ policies.
