Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 21, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Meji: Brain Training (“we,” “our,” or “us”) handles information when you use our mobile application (“Application”), available on both Apple App Store (iOS) and Google Play Store (Android).

This policy applies equally to all users of the Application, regardless of platform. By using the Application, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information (PII), such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or contact details.

All gameplay progress, scores, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device only. This data is never uploaded to our servers and is not accessible to the Developer.

We may collect limited non-personal information automatically, including:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, language settings, and performance data.
  • Advertising Identifiers: Used by advertising services to deliver relevant or non-personalized ads.

2. Third-Party Services

The Application may use third-party services that collect data independently:

  • Google AdMob – Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, advertising IDs, and usage data.

All purchases and subscriptions on iOS are processed securely through Apple In-App Purchase. The Developer does not have access to users’ payment information.
