Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 21, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Meji: Brain Training (“we”, “our”, or “us”) handles information when you use our mobile application (“Application”), available on both Apple App Store (iOS) and Google Play Store (Android).

This policy applies equally to all users, regardless of platform. By using the Application, you agree to the practices described below.

We do not collect, store, or process personally identifiable information such as name, email address, phone number, or contact details.

All gameplay progress, scores, preferences, and settings are stored locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to our servers and cannot be accessed by the Developer.

Limited non-personal data may be collected automatically by third-party services:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, language settings, and basic performance data.
